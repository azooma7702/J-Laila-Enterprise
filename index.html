About J'Laila Enterprise


Since 2016

The owner of this establishment is Puan Umie Laila Binti Muhammad, a 35-year-old Muslim and a mother of two children. Her fervent love for baking and decorating cakes led her to establish this home-based enterprise in 2016. With her extensive background in cake artistry, it's hardly a shock that she has amassed a loyal customer base who rely on her exceptional baking skills.

Umie Laila has made a concerted effort to use premium ingredients and proven baking methods, among other things as well to fills her customer satisfaction.
